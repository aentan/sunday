Walk for Water

Every step counts.

Based on the number of steps you take, a brand/sponsor decides to donate a value to Charity Water. Walk for Water is designed to keep track of the steps taken by event attendees for the purpose of generating donations, but anyone can use the app whenever they wish to measure their activity level.

The app automatically tracks your activity in the background. If you’d like to turn off tracking completely, tap the on/off toggle in the sidebar.

Without an internet connection, the app collects data and then analyses it after reconnection. If you travel abroad and don't have a data connection, your iPhone’s location services may be less accurate until you connect to a network.
