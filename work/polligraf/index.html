Polligraf

Ask any question. Get a beautiful answer.

Polligraf is a simple new tool that lets users poll their social networks, and generate beautiful infographics with the results.

Polligraf generates infographics in real time. And it allows users to chose the infograhic filter of their choice (free & paid) like selecting photo filters on Instagram.
